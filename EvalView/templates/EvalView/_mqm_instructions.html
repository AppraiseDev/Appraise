• Marking spans:
    • Highlight parts of the text with your cursor.
    • Click the span repeatedly to increase its severity.
  • Severity:
    • Critical: Errors that drastically change the meaning or make the content incomprehensible.
    • Major: Errors that cause a change in meaning or a significant reduction in fluency, but do not make the text incomprehensible.
    • Minor: Less severe errors that do not significantly change the meaning or reduce the fluency of the text. They might be small mistakes in grammar, spelling, or punctuation that do not greatly affect the overall quality of the translation.
    • Neutral: Used to note aspects of the translation that are not necessarily errors but might be points of interest, such as regional language variations or stylistic choices.
    • Undecided: Only used as an unfinished placeholder. Click the selected span to change its severity.
